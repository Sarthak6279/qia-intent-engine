FAQ

Common questions

Mixpanel and Amplitude analyze what happened yesterday and surface it in a dashboard for humans to review. QIA predicts what will happen in the next 30–60 seconds and triggers automated actions without human intervention. The key difference: timing and automation. We act during the decision window — they act after the window closes.
No. QIA uses a weighted behavioral scoring model grounded in UX psychology research — not machine learning. It works from your very first event with zero training data. As you collect data over time, you can optionally tune the scoring thresholds via our API to improve accuracy for your specific use case.
When a user's intent score exceeds 75, our backend calls the QIAIncentive.sol smart contract's triggerIncentive() function via ethers.js. The contract emits an IncentiveTriggered event on-chain with the user's wallet address, a timestamp, and an incrementing incentive ID. This creates a permanent, immutable record that can be queried by your frontend, used for airdrops, or verified by auditors.
Most teams are tracking events within 5 minutes. Step 1: Include qia-sdk.js in your HTML. Step 2: Call QIA.init() with your API key. Step 3: Call QIA.track() wherever you want to capture behavior. The full documentation covers advanced use cases like scroll tracking, wallet delay measurement, and hover hesitation patterns.
Our smart contract is EVM-compatible and can be deployed to any Ethereum-compatible network — Ethereum mainnet, Sepolia testnet, Polygon, Arbitrum, Avalanche, Base, and more. You configure your target network via the RPC_URL environment variable in the backend. Multi-chain support (triggering across multiple chains simultaneously) is on our roadmap for Q4 2026.
In the current MVP, event data is processed in-memory and not persisted beyond the scoring calculation. We do not store behavioral data beyond what's needed to compute the score. In the production roadmap, event logs are stored per-customer in isolated databases, never shared, and fully deletable. GDPR and privacy compliance tooling is on our Q3 2026 roadmap.
